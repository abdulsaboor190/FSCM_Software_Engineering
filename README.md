# FAST Sports Complex Management System (FSCMS)

A centralized web-based platform designed to digitize and automate sports facility management at FAST-NUCES Karachi. The system enables students, faculty, coaches, and administrators to efficiently manage facility bookings, tournaments, equipment inventory, and analytics through a unified interface.

---

# Project Overview

The FAST Sports Complex currently handles bookings, tournaments, and equipment management through manual or fragmented processes. This often leads to scheduling conflicts, inefficient resource tracking, and lack of transparency.

The **FAST Sports Complex Management System (FSCMS)** aims to solve these problems by providing a modern digital solution that integrates all sports management activities into a single platform.

---

# Key Features

- Facility reservation with time-slot management
- Real-time facility availability checking
- Booking conflict detection
- Tournament creation and match scheduling
- Leaderboards and score tracking
- Equipment inventory management
- Equipment check-in / check-out system
- Coach session scheduling
- Analytics dashboard for facility usage and revenue
- Issue reporting and maintenance tracking
- Role-based authentication system

---

# System Architecture

The project follows a **3-Layer Architecture**:

**Presentation Layer**
- Web-based user interface for students, coaches, and administrators.

**Application Layer**
- Booking engine
- Tournament management
- Equipment tracking
- Analytics module
- Notification service

**Data Layer**
- Centralized relational database for storing users, bookings, tournaments, and equipment records.
